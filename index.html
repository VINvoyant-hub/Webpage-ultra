How it works

Not “diagnose my car over text.” More like: a disciplined evidence pipeline that turns your chaos into a ranked plan.

01 • Pay + Claim Ticket

Pay first (Stripe). You get a Request ID on the intake page—your universal tag. Put it in folder names, messages, and receipts. Evidence stays married to the ticket.

02 • Submit Intake

VIN + symptoms + what changed recently. If you have codes, include them. If you have screenshots, include them. The goal is a clean narrative—not a novel.

03 • Upload Evidence

Scan screenshots, a 20–30s video, a few targeted photos. Remote diagnosis is an evidence game. We play it properly.

04 • Get the Plan

You receive a structured response: ranked causes, what to test next, risk level, parts options across major sources, and what each outcome means.

FAQ

Clear answers. No mysticism. No “replace the ECU and pray.”

Can you “diagnose” without seeing the car?

We can’t touch your car, but we can do what good techs do: build a hypothesis tree and destroy the wrong branches with evidence. The better the evidence, the fewer wasted parts and guesses.

Is VINvoyant only for Mercedes/AMG?

It’s built from a Mercedes/AMG brain, but the method is universal. VIN-based maintenance logic, evidence triage, and parts sanity checks work across platforms.

Do you buy parts for me or deliver them?

VINvoyant focuses on guidance and pricing intelligence. In some cases, we can help you plan logistics (pickup/delivery services), but availability depends on your area and providers.

What’s the fastest way to get a great answer?

Send scan screenshots (codes + freeze frame), a 20–30s video of the symptom, and tell us what changed recently. That combo turns fog into structure.

What if I don’t have a scan tool?

Still submit. Describe symptoms precisely and upload photos/video. We’ll tell you the highest-value next test (often cheap) and what to ask for if you visit a shop.

Is payment verified before submission?

This single-page version uses Stripe for payment and Netlify for intake. If you want true payment verification (server-confirmed), switch to the Functions build. Same vibe—stronger gate.
